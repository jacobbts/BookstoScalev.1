FAQ

Your Questions, Answered.

What makes Books To Scale Corporation different from other accounting firms?+
Two things set Books To Scale Corporation apart: a belief that people and systems drive results, along with a strong focus on strategy and clients. Most accounting firms are reactive, they file what happened. We plan what happens next. Our team includes licensed real estate professionals, which means we understand property transactions, portfolio structuring, and the tax implications of real estate decisions at a level most general practitioners simply can't match. We work exclusively with growing professionals, physicians, attorneys, real estate agents, investors, and business owners, whose financial lives require more than a template. Every engagement at Books To Scale Corporation is built around where you are and where you're headed.
Do you handle personal taxes as well as business taxes?+
Yes, and for most of our clients, one affects the other closely. Growing professionals operate in a world where their business income, personal income, real estate holdings, and investment activity are all deeply interconnected. A decision made at any level can have direct personal tax implications, and vice versa. Books To Scale Corporation manages both sides of that picture with a unified strategy, so nothing falls through the cracks and every dollar is accounted for in the right place. Our job is to handle the financial complexity so you can focus on what you do best: your craft, your clients, your practice.
What is the difference between tax planning and tax preparation?+
Tax preparation is looking back. Tax planning is looking forward, and it's the difference between arriving in April with a surprise or arriving with a strategy. The best tax returns don't happen by accident. They are the product of decisions made throughout the year: how income is timed and categorized, how entities are structured, how real estate transactions are positioned, and how major financial events, an inheritance, a property sale, a large gift, are planned for in advance. At Books To Scale Corporation, planning is not a separate service from preparation. It is the process that makes preparation matter. The two work together to achieve the financial outcomes our clients are building toward.
What does a CFO Analysis include?+
Growing professionals are exceptional at what they do, and that focus is exactly why the financial details beneath the surface often go unnoticed. A real estate broker may not see the margin compression happening across their transaction volume. A physician may not realize how much their billing cycle is affecting cash flow. A business owner may not be tracking the KPIs that actually drive profitability and scalability. Books To Scale Corporation's CFO Analysis is designed to surface what gets lost beneath the day-to-day. It delivers ratio analysis, KPI breakdowns, and an operations-level financial review, the kind of insight that shows you what is actually happening in your business, not just what the P&L says. It is the financial perspective that most growing professionals never had access to before.
Can I mix and match services?+
Yes. Books To Scale Corporation's services are designed to work independently or together, and many clients begin with one or two services and expand over time. That said, the most powerful results come when we are managing the full picture — books, taxes, planning, and analysis working in coordination. When your accounting feeds your tax strategy, and your tax strategy drives your results, everything compounds. We are always happy to start where you are, but our goal is always to build toward a comprehensive relationship that gives your financial life the full attention it deserves.
Do you work with clients remotely?+
Yes. Books To Scale Corporation is based in Sacramento, California, and serves clients across many states. We work with real estate professionals, physicians, attorneys, and business owners throughout the country who value the same level of personalized service regardless of geography. Our client base is constantly growing, and we are set up to deliver the same high-touch experience remotely that we provide locally. Distance is never a barrier to great financial strategy.
How does onboarding work?+
Books To Scale Corporation schedules a consultation with you, where we review materials together, take the time to understand your current financial situation, your entities, your real estate holdings if applicable, and, most importantly, where your needs currently lie and where you want to go. From that conversation, we build a plan. Not a template. A plan shaped around your practice, your goals, and the financial life you are building. Onboarding is not a form to fill out. It is the beginning of a working relationship.
